UCChat Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: April 16, 2026

How to Delete Your Data

You can delete your UCChat account and all associated data by following these steps:

  1. Open the UCChat app
  2. Go to Menu (bottom right tab)
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Enter your username to confirm
  5. Tap Delete — your account and all messages will be permanently deleted

What Gets Deleted

  • Your profile information (name, email, student ID, photo)
  • All your messages and conversations
  • Your account credentials
